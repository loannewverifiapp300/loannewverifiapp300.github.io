How to start?

Get started

  • Create account

    To become an active investor of coin-traders.uk you need to create a personal account at our website first. Click the button ‘register’ and fill in several mandatory fields. We do not demand to confirm your registration via SMS or e-mail as we are certain that investors will make sure to check if they have filled everything in correctly and that they are not going to create fake accounts.

    sign up sign up

  • Make Deposit

    After account registration you will get the possibility to choose between investment plans and deposit. Define the plan you like the most and choose one of the ways of payment that is the most convenient to you. We recommend you think well and decide on how you wish to invest your money before even registering at the website to make the time period between choosing investment plan and depositing money as less as possible.
